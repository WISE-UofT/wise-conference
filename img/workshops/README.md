This folder contains images for the workshop blog cards on the conference website.

Please add the following images to display workshop photos:

9. `academic-journey.jpg` - For "Mapping your Academic Journey" workshop

## Image Specifications

- **Recommended Size**: 800x600 pixels (or similar 4:3 aspect ratio)
- **Format**: JPG or JPEG (you can also use PNG or WebP)
- **Orientation**: Landscape (horizontal)
- **File Size**: Optimize to keep under 500KB per image for faster loading

## Notes

- Images will display at 200px height in the card meta section
- The photo will zoom slightly (1.3x) and rotate (3deg) on hover
- Images should be relevant to the workshop topic and visually engaging
- Consider using stock photos related to: data analysis, innovation, AI/technology, leadership, entrepreneurship, aerospace, brain imaging, interviews, and academic settings
